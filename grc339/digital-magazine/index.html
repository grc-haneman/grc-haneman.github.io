History of Coffee

Originating from the ancient coffee forests of Ethiopia, the history of coffee dates back to the 9th century, where legend has it that a goat herder named Kaldi discovered the energizing properties of coffee beans. Since then, coffee has evolved into a global phenomenon, with its cultivation, trade, and cultural significance weaving a rich narrative that spans centuries and continents.
